The Facts

One of the first facts most people think about when they hear starfish is the fact that they can regrow lost limbs. A neat add on to this fact is that there have been cases where starfish have regrown their entire bodies just from a detached limb! Another cool fact is that there are over 2000 species (some listed below) of starfish discovered so far! They are also capable of living up to 35 years!

List of Some Starfish Species

  • Crown of Thorns Starfish
  • Blue Sea Star
  • Protoreaster Nodosus
  • Astropecten Articulatus
